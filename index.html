DUNGEONERS

When the world needs a hero, only you answered the call...

The Story

The Alliance of Kingdoms was at peace, until one day, an otherworldly being started to spawn from the sky, corrupting our peoples' minds. It nearly destroyed The Alliance, one Faction blaming the other, until our leader, King Maximus, started the investigation and set an army to investigate the phenomenon.

The Brave

You are called by King Maximus to investigate the ruins that contain relics of the past, so that we can be free of the beings.

The Journey

With your trusty blade, handy bow, and fierce pet, you begin your journey to the ruins throughout the continent to save The Alliance, and to save the world.
